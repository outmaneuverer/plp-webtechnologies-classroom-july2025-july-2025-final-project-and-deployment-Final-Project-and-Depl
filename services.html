Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a farm tour? +

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for weekend tours, and 1 week for weekday tours. For groups larger than 10 people, please contact us at least 3 weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Can I customize my CSA box? +

Yes! Our CSA program offers customization options. You can specify preferences, allergies, and even swap items you don't prefer for alternatives when available through our online portal.

What happens if it rains on the day of my tour? +

We have covered areas and indoor spaces where tours can continue comfortably. In case of severe weather, we'll contact you to reschedule or provide a full refund if rescheduling isn't possible.

Are your workshops suitable for beginners? +

Absolutely! Our workshops are designed for all skill levels. We indicate the difficulty level in each workshop description, and many are perfect for beginners with no prior experience.
