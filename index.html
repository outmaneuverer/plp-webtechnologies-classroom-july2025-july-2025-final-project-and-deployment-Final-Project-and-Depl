Sustainable Farming Since 2008

Fresh Harvest Farm began as a small family operation with a simple mission: to provide the freshest, healthiest food while caring for our land and animals.

Today, we're proud to be one of the region's leading sustainable farms, combining traditional farming wisdom with modern eco-friendly practices.

Our integrated farming system allows us to raise fish, poultry, and vegetables in harmony, creating a natural cycle that benefits both our products and the environment.
