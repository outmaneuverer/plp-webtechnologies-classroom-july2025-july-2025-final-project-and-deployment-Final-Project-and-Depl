About Fresh Harvest Farm

Rooted in tradition, grown with care, and harvested sustainably for your family’s table.

Our Journey

2008

Fresh Harvest Farm was founded as a small family operation with a mission to provide fresh, healthy food while protecting the environment.

2015

We expanded our operations to include aquaculture, raising fish in a sustainable, eco-friendly system.

2020

Launched farm tours, workshops, and community programs to share sustainable farming knowledge.

2025

Recognized as a leading sustainable farm in the region, with fish, poultry, and organic vegetables raised in harmony.
