Frequently Asked Questions

What are your farm store hours? +

Our farm store is open Monday through Saturday from 9am to 6pm, and Sundays from 10am to 4pm. We're closed on major holidays.

Do I need to make an appointment to visit? +

No appointment is needed to visit our farm store. However, for guided tours, workshops, and special events, we recommend booking in advance to secure your spot.

Do you deliver products? +

Yes, we offer local delivery within a 20-mile radius for orders over $50. We also partner with regional delivery services for farther locations. Delivery fees vary based on distance.

Can I volunteer at the farm? +

We welcome volunteers! Please contact us to learn about our volunteer programs and scheduled work days. No prior farming experience is necessary.
